# Rise & Move Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2026

Rise & Move does not collect, store, or share personal data.

## Data Collection
Rise & Move does not collect any personal information from users. The app does not require account creation or sign-in.

## Subscriptions
Rise & Move offers optional paid subscriptions that are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. Rise & Move does not have access to users’ payment information.

## Notifications
Rise & Move uses local notifications to deliver alarm functionality. Notification permissions are requested only to support app features.

## Tracking
Rise & Move does not track users and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Data Sharing
No user data is shared with third parties.

## Children’s Privacy
Rise & Move does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
